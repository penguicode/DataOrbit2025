Welcome to our hackathon project! We are a group of 4 passionate and dedicated individuals at our first hackathon.

Our project, FireGuard, aims to present a visual representation of past wildfires in California as well as the property value and fire insurance. With various maps, we hope to showcase the risks of wildfires and create a tool that can empower individuals with the knowledge to make informed decisions when looking at different locations based on wildfires, property value, and fire insurance. We were inspired by the recent devastating Palisade fires and believe that our solution can help raise awareness about the risk of wildfires. Throughout this hackathon, we have collaborated, brainstormed, and coded tirelessly to bring our vision to life.

We are excited to showcase our interactive map and look forward to seeing how it can benefit California residents. Thank you for visiting our page and taking the time to learn more about our project. Your support and feedback mean the world to us.
